OVERVIEW

ResilientCare is an AI Driven Mental Health Coach which specializes in day to day conflicts that helps handle your emotions in any MICRO situation. Students specifically often struggle with Daily Friction. Daily Friction is defined as conflicts that aren't classified as a mental crisis.

01

Open the app, hold the Vent button

Whenever you're overwhelmed, anxious, or just need to be heard, open ResilientCare and press the glowing orb. Speak freely for as long as you need.

02

Your AI listens with care

Our compassionate AI processes your words with empathy reflecting back thoughtful responses that help you feel understood, not analysed.

03

Grow through Insight reflections

After each session, review emotional insights tailored to you. Watch patterns emerge, celebrate breakthroughs, and gently identify areas to improve.
